Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide a better experience, remember your login session, and ensure secure video calls.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you signed in during your video call session.
  • Security: To protect your data and prevent unauthorized access to your rooms.
  • Performance: To ensure the WebRTC signaling works smoothly between participants.

3. Essential Cookies Only

ToxicLabs IGVC does not use tracking or advertising cookies. We only use "Strictly Necessary" cookies that are required for the website to function.

4. Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that if you disable essential cookies, the video call features and login system will not work.

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us via Telegram: @toxic_io
