Privacy Policy

At ToxicLabs, we prioritize your privacy. We do not store your video calls or audio data.

1. Data Collection

We only collect basic account info (Email/Instagram ID) for authentication. We do not track your IP or location.

2. Encryption

All calls are Peer-to-Peer (P2P) and End-to-End Encrypted using WebRTC technology. Even ToxicLabs cannot see your calls.

3. Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We use official Meta OAuth for secure Instagram logins.
