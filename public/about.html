About ToxicLabs

A privacy-first tech lab dedicated to building secure, beautiful, and simplified communication tools for the modern web.

Our Story

The Instagram Video Call (IGVC) project was born out of a simple idea: Why should secure video calling be complicated? We wanted to bridge the gap between your social identity and high-end encryption.

Developed by Toxic, this platform ensures that you can connect face-to-face with anyone, instantly, without worrying about data leaks or invasive tracking.

Why Choose IGVC?

🔒 Zero Tracking We don't store logs, IPs, or call history. Ever.
🚀 No Downloads Works directly in your browser using WebRTC.
🥀 IG Integration Official Meta OAuth for trusted, instant login.
